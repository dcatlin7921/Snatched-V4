What is redaction?

Redaction strips sensitive metadata from your output files before you download or share them. GPS coordinates, creator names, and timestamps can reveal personal information — remove them here.

Non-destructive: Only the output copies are modified. Your pipeline data (matches, GPS trail, etc.) is untouched. All changes are logged in the audit trail below.
