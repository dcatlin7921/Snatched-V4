What To Know
info

Snapchat compresses your data

Snapchat's data export delivers photos at lower quality than the originals saved to your camera roll. Files exported from Snapchat's servers are pre-compressed (typically JPEG quality 61–80), and data has been shown to shrink over time as Snapchat re-encodes stored media. Snatched preserves every byte Snapchat gives us — we never re-compress your photos. Overlays (text, stickers) are burned at quality 95 to minimize any additional loss. The sooner you export, the better your quality will be.
